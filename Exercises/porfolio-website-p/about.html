About Me

Hi! I'm Dario Goncalves. I'm a graphic designer live in Massachusetts, USA. I started loving graphic design when I was in middle school. It's not just a job for me, it's something I really enjoy.

I like starting with a blank page and making it into something cool. I make things like book covers, flyers, and album covers. I also like editing pictures and trying out new ideas. When I work on these things, it feels like I'm in my own fun world. And when I finish a project, that is when i feel satisfied.

Every design has its own story, and I want to help tell yours. If you need someone to make designs for you, I'm here to help. You can email or call me. Thanks for reaching out
